By default, peaks are ordered by p-value (t-tests or ANOVA). To perform functional analysis on a peak cluster,
  1. From the top menu bar, select a method under Cluster peaks;
  2. From the left panel, drag-and-select a region of interest;
  3. From the right panel, set a database and click Submit button;
  4. From the result table, click a row to show pathway annotations (color squares); Note: not all matching compounds are displayed. For more details, click on the annotation of interest.
